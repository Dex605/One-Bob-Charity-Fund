⭐ Featured Parent Testimony

My son Kamau is eight years old. Every morning he would leave for school without eating anything — I had nothing to give him. His teacher told me he was falling asleep in class. I felt like I had failed him. Then One Bob Charity Fund came to his school. They brought food, a school bag, notebooks and a uniform. Kamau came home that day and said — Mama, someone cares about me. I cried for one hour. Someone cares about my child.

W
Wambui Njoroge
🇰🇪 Nairobi, Kenya · Mother of Kamau, Age 8
