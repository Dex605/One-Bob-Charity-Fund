Who We Are

Built on one belief —
every child matters

One Bob Charity Fund was born from personal pain and a simple conviction: that small acts of kindness, multiplied by many hearts, can change the lives of children who have been forgotten by the system.

What Drives Us

Our mission & vision

Two simple statements that guide every decision we make, every delivery we carry out, and every child we help.

🎯
Our Mission
To ensure no child in East Africa goes without food, education, or clothing because of poverty.

We collect small donations from many people and use every shilling to directly buy and deliver essential items to the most vulnerable children in Kenya, Tanzania, and Uganda. No child is turned away. No donation is too small.

🌍
Our Vision
A generation of East African children who grow up knowing they are not alone.

We believe that a child who is fed, clothed, and educated becomes an adult who builds their community. Our vision is not just to help children survive — it is to help them thrive and become the change East Africa needs.

What We Stand For

Our core values

These values are not words on a wall. They are the rules we live by in every decision, every delivery, and every interaction.

🤝
Transparency

Every donation is tracked. Every delivery is photographed. Every shilling is accounted for. We show you exactly where your money goes because we have nothing to hide.

💛
Dignity

We deliver help with love and respect. Every child we serve deserves to feel seen and valued — not pitied. We treat every family the way we would want our own families to be treated.

🎯
Direct Impact

No middlemen. No big offices. Our team personally buys and delivers to children. When you donate, your bob reaches a child — not an administrator's salary.

🌱
Community First

We buy from local suppliers to keep money circulating in East African communities. We work with local schools and community leaders who know the children best.

🔥
Urgency

A child who misses a meal today loses brain development that cannot be recovered. A child who drops out of school today loses a future. We act fast because children cannot wait.

🌍
Inclusion

We do not choose children based on tribe, religion, or gender. Every vulnerable child in our three countries is equal in our eyes. Poverty does not discriminate and neither do we.

The Person Behind It All

Meet our founder

Harvey Dex · Founder & CEO

Born with nothing.
Built with everything.

Harvey Dex · Founder & CEO · One Bob Charity Fund

I grew up in a home where love was present but money was not. My mother ran a small shop — her hands and that little counter were the only things standing between our family and hunger. She fed us, clothed us, and somehow found a way to keep me in school. My father tried too, in his own way. But alcohol had a grip on him that was stronger than anything we could say or do. He lost jobs. He sold our land piece by piece. We watched and we hoped, and we kept going.

I finished secondary school. That alone felt like a miracle.

When I got to college, there was no money waiting for me. So I found a cleaning job — KSh 9,000 a month — and a part-time job that added KSh 6,000 more. With that KSh 15,000 I paid my rent, bought my food, bought my clothes, and sat for my second year exams. Nobody knew. I just kept going.

In my third year I took a security job. I worked nights. I studied when I could. I saved everything I could. Then my father called — he had sold the last piece of land we owned and promised to pay for my final exams and teacher training fees. I resigned. I came home full of hope.

He changed his mind. He told me the land was his, the money was his, and he could do whatever he wanted with it. That day something broke inside me — and something else was born.

I left with the little I had saved, learned computer skills, and kept thinking about all the children I had seen along the way. Children sitting in classrooms hungry. Children walking barefoot to school. Children who were one small act of kindness away from a completely different future.

I started One Bob Charity Fund because I know what it feels like to be that child. And I know what it feels like when someone believes in you — even with just a little. My dream is that one day the children we help will grow up, build careers, and come back to give to the next generation. A cycle of kindness that never stops.

If you are reading this — you are already part of that cycle. Thank you.
