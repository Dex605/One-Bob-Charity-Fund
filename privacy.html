Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025 · One Bob Charity Fund

Your privacy matters to us. One Bob Charity Fund is a charity that collects small donations to help children in Kenya, Tanzania and Uganda. When you donate, contact us, or use our website, you may share personal information with us. This policy explains clearly what we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it. We keep it simple — no legal jargon.

01
📋 What information we collect

We only collect information that you choose to give us. This may include:

  • Your name — when you donate or contact us
  • Your email address or phone number — when you subscribe, donate, or fill in a form
  • Your country — when you donate internationally, so we can recognise you properly
  • Your donation amount and payment reference — to verify and record your contribution
  • Basic browser and device data — automatically collected when you visit our website (IP address, browser type, pages visited)

We do not store your card or M-Pesa PIN details. Payments go through secure third-party processors.

02
🎯 Why we collect it

We use your information only for the following purposes:

  • To process and record your donation and confirm it was received
  • To recognise you in our donation ticker and Top Donors page if applicable
  • To send you updates about deliveries and children you have helped — only if you opted in
  • To respond to your messages when you contact us
  • To improve our website using anonymous browsing statistics
03
🔒 How we protect your information

We take the security of your personal information seriously:

  • We never sell your personal information to anyone
  • We never share your data with third parties except where necessary to process your donation (e.g. PayPal, Binance)
  • Your data is stored securely and only accessible to the OBCF team
  • Our website uses HTTPS encryption to protect data in transit
04
🍪 Cookies

Our website uses basic cookies to help it function properly and to understand how visitors use the site. These cookies do not identify you personally. They help us see which pages are most visited and how donors navigate the site so we can improve the experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time — the site will still work.

05
🌍 International donors

If you are donating from outside East Africa — for example from the UK, Germany, Canada or the USA — your data is handled with the same care as all other donors. We collect your country of origin purely to recognise your generosity in our ticker and Top Donors page. We do not share this with any government or third party. Third-party payment services such as PayPal and Binance Pay have their own privacy policies which we encourage you to review.

06
✋ Your rights

You are in control of your personal data at all times:

  • You can ask us what data we hold about you at any time
  • You can ask us to delete your data from our records
  • You can opt out of our email updates at any time by contacting us
  • You can request to be shown as anonymous in our ticker and donor pages

To exercise any of these rights, simply contact us at info@onebobcharityfund.org

07
🔄 Changes to this policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the new version on this page with the updated date at the top. We encourage you to check back occasionally. Continued use of the website after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.

Have a privacy question?

We are a small, transparent team and we are happy to answer any question you have about how we handle your data. Just reach out.
