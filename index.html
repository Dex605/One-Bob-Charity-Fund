Simple & Transparent

How your bob reaches
a child

Every shilling donated goes directly to buying and delivering what children need most. No big offices. No waste. Just real impact.

💳
Step 01
You Donate Any Amount
Even one shilling counts. Every donation is recorded and goes into the fund immediately.
🛒
Step 02
We Buy What is Needed
Our team buys food, school supplies, and clothing from local suppliers — keeping money in the community.
🚐
Step 03
We Deliver Directly
We travel to schools and communities. No middlemen, no delays — straight to the children who need it.
📸
Step 04
We Show You the Impact
Every delivery is photographed and shared. You will see exactly which children your bob helped.

Our Founder

The story behind One Bob

Harvey Dex
Founder & CEO · One Bob Charity Fund

I grew up in a home where love was present but money was not. My mother ran a small shop — her hands and that little counter were the only things standing between our family and hunger. I finished secondary school, worked as a cleaner and a security guard to pay my own college rent, and saved every shilling I could. When the moment I had worked toward was taken from me by someone I trusted, something broke inside me — and something else was born. I started One Bob Charity Fund because I know what it feels like to be the child that the world forgot. And I know what one small act of kindness can do.
