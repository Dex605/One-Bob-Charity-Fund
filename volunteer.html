Why Join Us

What you get from volunteering

Volunteering with One Bob Charity Fund is not just about giving — it is about growing, connecting, and being part of something real.

💛
Real Impact

You will personally see children receive food, clothing, and school supplies. This is not abstract charity work — you will witness the difference you make with your own eyes.

🌍
Community & Connection

Join a growing team of passionate people across East Africa who believe every child matters. Build friendships and connections that last beyond the work.

📈
Grow Your Skills

Whether you are in logistics, social media, photography, or community work — you will gain real hands-on experience that looks excellent on any CV or portfolio.

📜
Certificate of Service

Every volunteer receives a formal certificate of service from One Bob Charity Fund recognising their contribution and time commitment.

🤝
Flexible Commitment

We understand you have a life. Volunteer on weekends, remotely, or during specific delivery trips. We work around your schedule — not the other way around.

🔥
Be Part of History

One Bob Charity Fund is just beginning. Get in early and help shape what we become. The people who join now will be the foundation of something that outlasts all of us.
